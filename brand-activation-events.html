What it is

A platform built for
the music industry.

Meet Me at the Hotel is a hospitality-driven B2B platform for the global music industry, hosted at Room Mate Aitana Hotel in Amsterdam during ADE week. It is not a traditional conference — it is a curated environment where industry professionals connect naturally over five days.

The platform combines open networking through the Industry Hub, curated business meetings through the Industry Exchange, artist showcases via the Oscar Room, and brand activations through partner programmes.

Hotel guests receive full access throughout the week. Registered industry professionals can access the Hub via RSVP. October 20–25, 2026 · Room Mate Aitana Hotel · IJdok 6 · Amsterdam.

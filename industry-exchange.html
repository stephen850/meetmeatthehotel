What is the Industry Exchange?
The right
partners.
Guaranteed.

The Meet Me Industry Exchange is Layer 02 of the MMATH ecosystem — the curated business meeting program. Participants apply to join and are matched with relevant partners based on their business objectives.

Instead of random networking, the Exchange connects the right people in a focused business environment. You apply, we review, we match — and you walk into a week of meetings that actually matter.

Selected meetings take place in the MMATH Business Lounge inside Room Mate Aitana Hotel. Approximately 20 minutes per meeting. No badges, no chaos, no wasted time. Measurable business value per edition.

MMATH Business Lounge

The central
meeting space.

The MMATH Business Lounge is the dedicated environment for all Industry Exchange meetings. Located inside Room Mate Aitana Hotel, the lounge provides a premium setting where participants meet industry professionals and explore new partnerships.

Meetings take place at hosted tables throughout the programme — structured, private, and purposeful.

  • Located inside Room Mate Aitana Hotel · IJdok 6
  • ~20 minutes per curated meeting
  • Hosted tables — dedicated meeting setup
  • Access to MMATH Business Lounge included
  • Programme runs throughout October week

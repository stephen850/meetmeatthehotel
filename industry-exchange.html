C-Suite Session · What is the Industry Exchange

The senior
leadership layer.

The Industry Exchange is a one-day closed session designed exclusively for the people who lead the global music industry. Not panels. Not pitches. A curated environment where founders, CEOs and senior executives can speak openly about the forces shaping the industry — and connect with peers who understand the pressures of leadership.

This is the inaugural edition — created in direct response to demand from the MMATH community. After five years of MeetMeAtTheHotel, the question kept coming back: "Is there something more focused? Just for senior leadership?" This is that session.

Everything discussed in the room stays in the room.

Program

One day.
Five hours.

11:00
Doors & Welcome Coffee
Arrival at Room Mate Aitana Hotel. Informal introductions and coffee before the session opens.
11:30
Opening Session
A structured conversation on the state of the global music industry — moderated, intimate and candid. No slides. No presentations. Just the room.
12:30
Pre-Matched Meetings
Two rounds of curated 20-minute one-on-one meetings, selected by our team based on your profile and objectives.
13:15
Lunch
Seated lunch for all participants. No agenda — just the right people around the table. Included in your registration.
14:30
Roundtable Sessions
Three parallel closed-door roundtables on themes chosen by participants. Groups of 15. Chatham House rules apply throughout.
15:30
Drinks Reception
The session closes with an informal drinks reception. Often where the most valuable connections are made. Included in your registration.

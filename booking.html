FAQ

Good
questions.

Do I need separate registration to access MMATH as a hotel guest?
+
No. Hotel guests receive full MMATH Industry Hub access automatically upon check-in. No separate registration, no additional cost.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
+
Breakfast is not included in the base rate but is available from €25 per person. Served daily from 07:00 to 12:00. Can be added to your booking on request.
Is parking available at the hotel?
+
Yes. Parking is available at €40 per 24 hours. We strongly recommend booking in advance as availability is limited. Mention it in your inquiry and we'll arrange it.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
Full details provided at booking. MMATH works with Room Mate Aitana's standard cancellation policy. Flexible options available for early bookings — contact us directly.
Can I attend without staying at the hotel?
+
Yes — RSVP for the Industry Hub is available for registered industry professionals. However, hotel guests receive priority access and all included benefits throughout the full week.

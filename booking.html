FAQ

Good
questions.

Do I need a separate ADE badge to access MMATH?
+
No. Hotel guests receive full MMATH hub access automatically upon check-in. No separate badge, no additional cost.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
+
Breakfast is not included in the base rate but is available from €25 per person. Served daily from 07:00 to 12:00 at the hotel restaurant. Can be added to your booking on request.
When do ADE 2026 dates get confirmed?
+
ADE traditionally takes place in the third or fourth week of October. Exact 2026 dates will be confirmed by ADE. Register your interest to be notified the moment bookings open.
Is parking available at the hotel?
+
Yes. Parking is available at €40 per 24 hours. We strongly recommend booking in advance during ADE week as availability is limited. Mention it in your inquiry and we'll arrange it for you.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
Full details provided at booking. MMATH works with Room Mate Aitana's standard cancellation policy with specific ADE booking terms. Flexible options available for early bookings — contact us directly.
Can I attend without staying at the hotel?
+
Yes — day passes and evening showcase tickets are available for registered industry professionals. However, hotel guests receive priority access, full week programming and all included benefits that day pass holders do not.

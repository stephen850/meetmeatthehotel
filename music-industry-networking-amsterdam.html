What it is

Not a conference.
A hotel takeover.

Meet Me at the Hotel is the annual gathering point of the global music industry — hosted entirely within a single Amsterdam hotel during ADE week. Unlike traditional conferences with badges, panels and booths, MMATH is built around the organic networking that happens naturally in a hospitality environment.

The platform brings together booking agents, label managers, artist managers, festival programmers, brand partnerships teams, media companies and music technology founders — all under one roof, throughout an entire week.

The Industry Hub is the open networking layer: no pre-scheduled meetings required, no application needed. Show up, connect, build relationships. The right people are already there.

For professionals seeking curated meetings, the Industry Exchange offers a structured programme of pre-matched 20-minute sessions with relevant partners from across the global music industry.

The networking model that works

The most valuable conversations in the music industry don't happen on stage. They happen in hotel corridors, over coffee, at the bar. MMATH is engineered for exactly that — a week-long hospitality environment where the right people are already in the building.

Hotel guests receive automatic full access. Day visitors can register for access to the Industry Hub via RSVP. Either way, the platform is open, the environment is curated, and the industry shows up.

Trusted by leading platforms including 1001Tracklists, We Rave You, Bootshaus, SLAM!, Revealed, Dedicated Agency and 250K Studio.

ADE Week Context

The business hub
during ADE 2026.

Amsterdam Dance Event (ADE) is one of the world's leading electronic music conferences and festivals, taking place each October in Amsterdam. For the global dance music and electronic music industry, ADE week is the most concentrated period of networking, deal-making and collaboration in the calendar year.

Meet Me at the Hotel positions itself as the hospitality-driven networking layer of ADE week — not a traditional conference, but a curated environment where the industry gathers naturally over five days. Where ADE brings the whole city to life, MMATH brings the industry together in one building.

The platform is independent from ADE but aligned in timing, serving the same international professional community with a focus on relationship-building over programming.

What is Meet Me at the Hotel?
+
Meet Me at the Hotel (MMATH) is a B2B music industry networking platform hosted at Room Mate Aitana Hotel in Amsterdam during ADE week. It combines open networking, curated business meetings, artist showcases and brand activations — all within a hospitality-first environment.
Who attends MMATH?
+
MMATH is exclusively for B2B music industry professionals: booking agents, label managers, artist managers, festival programmers, brand partnerships teams, music technology founders, media platforms and music industry executives. All profiles are reviewed manually.
Do I need to book a hotel room to attend?
+
No. You can register for Industry Hub access as a day visitor. However, hotel guests receive automatic full access throughout the entire week, plus priority for showcases, boat events and other curated sessions. Booking a room is the recommended way to experience MMATH in full.
When is MMATH October 2026?
+
MMATH October 2026 runs from Tuesday 20 October to Sunday 25 October 2026 at Room Mate Aitana Hotel, IJdok 6, Amsterdam. The opening night Industry Mixer takes place on Tuesday 20 October from 18:00.
Is there a cost to attend?
+
Industry Hub access via RSVP is free for registered B2B professionals. The Industry Exchange curated meetings programme has a separate registration process. Hotel rooms are charged at October 2026 rates from €285/night.

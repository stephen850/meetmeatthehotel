THE PROGRAM.

Five days. Three formats. Eight worlds. One hotel. The full ADE experience — curated exclusively for the dance industry.

Program Structure

The Day Program

The hotel transforms into the industry's busiest meeting place from 9am. Industry panels, innovation talks, brand sessions and curated networking — all accessible to hotel guests and registered day pass holders.

  • Industry panels & keynotes in the Oscar Room
  • Innovation & AI sessions — Meet Me Innovation stage
  • Brand activations throughout the lobby
  • Curated matchmaking meetings — pre-booked
  • Creator studio sessions — live podcast & video
  • Coffee & networking — Aitana Lounge

The Evening Program

As the sun sets over the IJ, the energy shifts. The Oscar Room becomes Amsterdam's most exclusive showcase stage. Label nights, artist debuts and branded events run until the early hours — industry access only.

  • Oscar Room showcases — 132m², marina views
  • Label nights & branded showcase evenings
  • Aitana Lounge Bar — network drinks from 18:00
  • Rooftop & boat departures
  • Industry dinner series — invite only
  • After-sessions til 02:00

The Invite Only

A curated layer of programming accessible only to select guests. CEOs, label heads, investors and senior creatives. Smaller rooms. Deeper conversations. No cameras, no stage.

  • CEO & Executive roundtables — 12 person max
  • Investor sessions — closed door
  • Industry Dinner — 200 leaders, 5th anniversary
  • Label Office Hours — booking agent desk
  • Future of Dance Summit
  • Access via Hub Partner or Founding Partner tier
Program Pillars

Five pillars.
One week.

01

INDUSTRY

The business of dance music. Label deals, booking conversations, management strategy and the future of the industry — at the highest level.

02

INNOVATION

Music tech, AI, Web3 and the tools reshaping how music is made, distributed and monetised. The frontier of what's next.

03

BRAND

Where global brands meet electronic music culture. Partnerships, activations and the commercial layer of the dance ecosystem.

04

CREATOR

Podcasters, filmmakers, content studios and the people telling the story of electronic music. Live from the hotel floor.

05

NETWORKING

Curated, intentional, efficient. Pre-matched meetings, curated salons and the serendipity that only comes from being in the right place.

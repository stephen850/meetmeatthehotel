The Day Program

Industry conversations, meetings, networking and creator activity across the hotel. Open to approved hotel guests and verified day pass holders.

  • Industry conversations & keynotes — Oscar Room
  • Innovation sessions — Meet Me Innovation stage
  • Brand activations throughout the hotel
  • Industry Exchange meetings — pre-booked via the Exchange
  • Creator sessions — live podcast & video from the hotel floor
  • Open networking — Aitana Lounge · Industry Hub

The Night Program

Partner activations and curated experiences spread across all hotel venues. RSVP-based access — screened and approved by the MMATH team.

  • OSCAR ROOM — Partner showcases, label nights & branded evenings · 132m² marina stage
  • AITANA LOUNGE — Networking receptions, partner gatherings & branded activations from 18:00
  • HOTEL SUITES — Private industry meetings, hosted conversations & closed-door sessions
  • IJDOK — Boat events departing from the marina · 40 guests max · Private IJ canal experiences

The Private Access

Closed-door sessions, executive roundtables, invite-only dinners and private conversations for senior industry professionals. Smaller groups, deeper dialogue.

  • Executive roundtables — 12 person max
  • Investor sessions — closed door
  • Industry Dinner — 200 leaders, 5th anniversary
  • Label Office Hours — booking agent desk
  • By partnership agreement or invitation

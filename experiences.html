The showcase stage of MMATH

The Oscar Room serves as the primary stage of MeetMeAtTheHotel and hosts many of the platform's partner showcases and evening activations.

Located at ground level with panoramic views over the IJdok marina, the space combines natural daylight with professional event production. Floor-to-ceiling windows, LED video wall and professional sound and lighting capabilities.

The Oscar Room has hosted numerous industry showcases and gatherings during MeetMeAtTheHotel, bringing together artists, labels, media platforms and music industry professionals.

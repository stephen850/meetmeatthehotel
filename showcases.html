The Venue

The Oscar Room.
Nothing like it.

The Oscar Room is the crown jewel of Room Mate Aitana Hotel — a purpose-designed event space with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the IJ marina. Designed by Amsterdam studio 250K, it's one of the most visually striking venues in the city.

During ADE week, it becomes the industry's most coveted showcase stage. Labels, agencies and brands compete for dates months in advance. Once you've performed or hosted here, you understand why.

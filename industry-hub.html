The Open Networking Floor

The Industry Hub is the open networking environment at the heart of MMATH. It's where professionals from across the global music industry connect naturally — over coffee, in the lounge, at the bar, in a hotel corridor.

Informal meetings, introductions between artists and promoters, conversations between brands and creators. No agenda. No badge-swapping. Just the right people in the right place.

The Hub is the open layer of the MMATH ecosystem — accessible to all registered guests throughout the entire week.

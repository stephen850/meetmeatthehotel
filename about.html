01
The Idea Behind MeetMeAtTheHotel

Why it
exists.

The global music industry brings together artists, agencies, clubs, festivals, brands and innovators from around the world. While these communities regularly meet at events and performances, there are few environments specifically designed for meaningful industry interaction.

MeetMeAtTheHotel was created to provide such an environment — a place where professionals can meet, exchange ideas and develop collaborations within a hospitality-driven setting.

The concept is simple: concentrate the right people in one place, give them the right environment, and let the industry do what it does best.

Founded

MeetMeAtTheHotel launched its first edition in 2022. What began as a focused gathering of industry professionals has grown into an international platform with participants from over 45 countries.

02
A Different Kind of Industry Environment

Not a typical
conference.

Unlike traditional conferences or trade shows, MeetMeAtTheHotel takes place in a hospitality-driven environment. This setting encourages natural conversations, informal meetings and spontaneous collaborations between industry professionals.

While the platform may host talks, discussions or curated conversations, the atmosphere remains fundamentally different from a conventional conference environment.

By combining hospitality with industry interaction, MeetMeAtTheHotel creates a unique setting where relationships develop naturally — beyond the formality of conference halls, the intensity of festivals or the pressure of a traditional trade floor.

Not a conference. Not a club night. Something in between — and something much more valuable.

03
A Global Industry Community

Who is
in the room.

Each edition brings together a diverse group of professionals from across the global music industry. Participants include artists, booking agencies, festival organizers, club operators, brands, media platforms and technology innovators who all contribute to shaping the future of music culture.

The platform is international by design. With participants from over 45 countries, conversations at MeetMeAtTheHotel reflect the truly global nature of the dance music industry — and the opportunities that come with it.

The Venue

Room Mate
Aitana Hotel.

Located at IJdok 6, right on the IJ waterfront, Room Mate Aitana Hotel is one of Amsterdam's most distinctive design hotels. During October week, it becomes the nerve centre of the global dance industry.

The hotel's architecture — from the Oscar Room's marina-view stage to the Aitana Lounge Bar — creates the perfect backdrop for the industry's most important conversations. The venue is not incidental to the platform. It is part of it.

  • Oscar Room showcase venue — 132m², marina view
  • Aitana Lounge Bar — networking & evening events
  • 3 dedicated meeting rooms
  • Marina-side boat departure point
  • IJdok 6 · Central Amsterdam
  • 5 min walk from Amsterdam Centraal

The Venue

Room Mate
Aitana Hotel.

Located at IJdok 6, right on the IJ waterfront, Room Mate Aitana Hotel is one of Amsterdam's most distinctive design hotels. During ADE week, it becomes the nerve centre of the global dance industry.

Designed with bold aesthetics and a lively hospitality spirit, the hotel's architecture — from the Oscar Room's marina-view stage to the Aitana Lounge Bar — creates the perfect backdrop for the industry's most important conversations.

  • Oscar Room showcase venue — 132m², marina view
  • Aitana Lounge Bar — networking & evening events
  • 3 dedicated meeting rooms
  • Marina-side boat departure point
  • IJdok 6 · Central Amsterdam
  • 5 min walk from Amsterdam Centraal

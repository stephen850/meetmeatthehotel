The Oscar Room

Amsterdam's most intimate
industry showcase venue.

The Oscar Room at Room Mate Aitana Hotel is the showcase venue at the heart of MMATH. With a capacity of 132m², it is deliberately intimate — designed for curated industry audiences rather than mass-market events.

Each evening during MMATH week, the Oscar Room hosts a curated showcase or brand activation. These events bring together artists, labels, booking agencies and industry professionals in a focused, premium environment where music meets business.

The 100% sell-out rate across previous editions reflects the demand for this format: quality over quantity, curation over open access, meaningful moments over spectacle.

For more on how to host a showcase, visit the Partners page or explore the full Experiences programme.

Showcase Formats

LABEL NIGHTS

Who attends showcases

The right audience
every night.

Showcase audiences at MMATH are drawn directly from the wider platform community — hotel guests and registered industry professionals who are already on-site throughout the week. This means showcase audiences are genuinely industry-professional rather than general public.

Booking agents attend to discover new talent. Label managers attend to hear competing rosters. Festival programmers attend to scout new acts. Brand teams attend to identify partnership opportunities. Every night brings together the decision-makers who matter most.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the
showcases.

How do I get access to MMATH showcases?
+
Hotel guests receive priority access to showcase tickets. Day visitors registered for Industry Hub access can apply for tickets through partner channels. Some showcases require direct RSVP with the hosting partner.
Can my label or brand host a showcase?
+
Yes. MMATH collaborates with a limited number of partners to host showcase evenings at the Oscar Room. Contact the team via the Partners page to discuss formats, availability and partnership structures.
What is the Oscar Room capacity?
+
The Oscar Room is 132m² and is deliberately kept intimate to ensure a curated, industry-focused environment. This is not a large club venue — it is a premium showcase space designed for the right audience rather than maximum capacity.

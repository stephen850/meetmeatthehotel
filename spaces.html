The Environment

Inside the
MMATH venue.

MeetMeAtTheHotel takes place inside Room Mate Aitana, a waterfront hotel located in the heart of Amsterdam. Beyond the main event spaces, the venue includes several private rooms that can be used for focused business interactions throughout the week.

These spaces allow partners to combine public presence with private business activity — all within the MMATH environment.

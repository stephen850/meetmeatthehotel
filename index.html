The Meet Me Ecosystem

One platform.
Four layers.

MeetMeAtTheHotel is built around a simple idea: bringing the key players of the global music industry together in one environment.

The platform combines open networking environments, curated business meetings and partner-driven experiences that allow professionals to connect, collaborate and create new opportunities.

Layer 01

Meet Me
Industry Hub

Open networking ecosystem

The Meet Me Industry Hub is the open networking environment of the platform. Here, artists, promoters, booking agencies, brands and industry professionals meet naturally throughout the event.

The hub encourages spontaneous conversations, introductions and collaborations within a relaxed hospitality environment.

Informal Meetings Coffee Conversations Artist × Promoter Brand × Agency Industry Discussions

Layer 02

Meet Me Industry
Exchange

Curated meetings program

The Meet Me Industry Exchange is a curated business meeting program designed to facilitate meaningful industry connections. Participants apply to join the program and are matched with relevant partners based on their business interests.

Meetings take place inside the Business Lounge and typically last around 20 minutes.

The ecosystem behind MeetMeAtTheHotel.

MeetMeAtTheHotel is the annual gathering point of the Meet Me Universe — a global ecosystem of platforms exploring the forces shaping modern music culture — from technology and fashion to creators, culture and hospitality. Together these platforms form an international network connecting artists, brands, innovators and industry professionals.

The Concept

More than
an event.

MeetMeAtTheHotel is the central gathering point of a broader ecosystem called the Meet Me Universe.

Within this ecosystem, several thematic platforms explore the different forces shaping modern nightlife culture and the global music industry.

Each platform focuses on a specific area of the industry while remaining connected to the wider MMATH environment. Together they create a multidisciplinary space where music, technology, fashion, culture and hospitality intersect.
